# Product Overview

Earth Movers Training Academy is a web-based training management platform for heavy equipment operator certification.

## Core Features

- **Public Website**: Course catalog, gallery, contact forms, about pages
- **Student Portal**: Dashboard, profile, payment tracking, certificates, course progress
- **Admin Portal**: Course management, student enrollment, payments, expenses, enquiries, CMS, gallery management, reviews

## User Roles

- **Public Users**: Browse courses, submit enquiries, view gallery
- **Students**: Access enrolled courses, track progress, view certificates, manage payments
- **Admins**: Full system access for managing courses, students, content, and operations

## Key Modules

- Course management with multi-language support
- Student enrollment and document management
- Transaction tracking (income/expenses)
- Gallery management (images/videos)
- Review and enquiry systems
- Promotional content (partners, posts, links)
